About Me

I am a Assistant Professor at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Future Networks, Beijing Normal University Zhuhai. My research interests include spatiotemporal data mining, urban computing, and artificial intelligence.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Xidian University in 2023. From 2017 to 2023, I worked as an algorithm engineer at prestigious institutions including Microsoft Research Asia, DiDi Research, and JD Technology, gaining extensive industry experience in applying AI to real-world problems.

I have published over 10 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, led 1 National Natural Science Foundation project, participated in 1 NSFC project, 2 National Key R&D Program projects, and 2 Beijing municipal research projects. I have also filed 2 patents.
